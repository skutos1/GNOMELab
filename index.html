Regenerative Agriculture Microbiomes

Goal of this project was to understand the effectiveness of compost applications & its influence over the soil microbiome across a range of dry rangeland conditions throughout New Mexico

This project explores the impacts of urbanization on microbiomes & envrionmental health as compared to more rural or suburban areas
